About the shop and the area


Craving of desserts does not stop here! There are a wide variety of hot and cold desserts from this store alone and from comparing with a few of other stores, this store's desserts taste are by far the most local and authentic personally. The location is also not far away from the nearest MRT station, Yishun. Nearby this dessert shop is a famous Yong Tao Foo stall, a supermarket and a bunch of other shops that are further down the corridor. There is a link to food review regarding about this dessert shop and so far it has been positive.


Hot desserts (Recommended) : Tau Suan, BoBo-ChaCha, BurBur-Hitam

Cold Desserts (Recommended) : Ice Kachang, Ice Jelly, Cheng Tng, BoBo-ChaCha


What is so good about local desserts?

Singaporeans has evolved the culture and has been named food paradise by tourists and the locals. Its convenient, can eat on the go and its made from the most authentic ingredients. Especially for cold desserts, it gives the shiok feeling to the customers for that chilling satisfaction. You should try it too!
